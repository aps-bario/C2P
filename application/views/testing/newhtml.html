DRM Returnee Research Survey
Introduction
We recognise that there are many challenges for Christians as they return to their home countries. Our hope is that by gathering feedback from those who have already returned, we can better help those who are preparing to return. The information gathered by this form will be used only for this purpose. Please feel free to use an alias or nickname, if you want your answers to be anonymous. We sincerely thank you for taking the time to complete this survey.
